# Minimus

A vendor competing with Chainguard in hardened container images. It ships minimal near-zero-CVE images with FIPS builds, SLSA Level 3 provenance and SBOMs.

**Why it matters:** The hardened base image space now has more than one supplier, which matters for pricing and for avoiding lock-in on something as foundational as your base image. Worth pricing against Chainguard if you are buying.
