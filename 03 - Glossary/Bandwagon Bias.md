# Bandwagon Bias

The judge follows a stated majority opinion. Mention that most people prefer response B and the score moves toward B, regardless of quality.

**Why it matters:** It rules out some tempting eval designs, such as showing the judge previous votes or aggregate ratings to save cost. Each judgement should be independent, with no access to what other judges or users concluded.

**Where it came up:** [[04 LLM as Judge - Arkadip Basu]]

**Source:** [LLM-as-a-Judge survey, arXiv:2412.05579](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.05579)
